Hilton Grand Vacations Inc. — Condensed Consolidated Financial Statements (UNAUDITED)

Period ended March 31, 2025.

Note 7. Timeshare Financing Receivables

Gross timeshare financing receivables totaled $3,450 million, against which the Company recorded an allowance for loan losses of $380 million, producing net receivables of $3,070 million. The allowance coverage ratio was approximately 11.0% of gross receivables. Provision for loan losses for the quarter was $54 million.

Delinquency status (% of gross portfolio)

  • 30 to 59 days past due: 1.8%
  • 60 to 89 days past due: 1.1%
  • 90 days or more past due: 6.2%
  • Total delinquent: 9.1%

Annualized default rate was 7.4%. The weighted-average FICO score at origination for new loans was 720. Approximately 62% of the portfolio had FICO scores of 700 or higher at origination; 4% had FICO scores below 600. Average loan size was approximately $28,500 with a weighted-average contract term of 120 months and a weighted-average LTV of 87%.

Note 9. Securitization Activity

Securitized receivables totaled $2,120 million with retained interests of $260 million. The outstanding warehouse facility balance was $310 million. During the quarter, the Company issued a new $375 million securitization at a 92% advance rate with a weighted-average coupon of 6.35% and overcollateralization of approximately 14%.

Gain on sale for the period was $42 million, a margin of approximately 11.2% of originations. Originations totaled $375 million, approximately 54% of which were sales to existing owners. Tour flow for the quarter was 178,000 tours with VPG of $3,980. The contract rescission rate was 11.8%.
