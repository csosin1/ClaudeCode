How to Play

Cournot pricing: More seats from everyone = lower ticket price for all. Choose routes wisely.

Fixed vs variable costs: Insurance & crew run even when grounded. Parking saves some but not all.

C-checks: Mandatory heavy maintenance. Cash paid upfront but smoothed on P&L via amortization. Cash flow ≠ net income!

Fuel index: Watch it — spikes make parking attractive. Read trends in the sparkline.

Loans: Interest-only, balloon at maturity. Bankruptcy if you can't roll over. Keep D/E low.

Net worth = cash + aircraft book values + maintenance assets − debt.

Goal: Highest net worth after 40 quarters (1950–1959).
